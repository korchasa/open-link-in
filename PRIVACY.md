# Privacy Policy — Smart Links Opener

_Last updated: 2026-06-17_

Smart Links Opener is designed to collect **no personal data whatsoever**.

## Data collection

The app does **not** collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal
information. There is no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, no advertising, and
no network communication initiated by the app for data collection.

## Data stored locally

The app stores only your routing rules (a list of domain → browser mappings) and
window position, kept **locally on your Mac**:

- Open-source (Developer ID) build: in macOS user preferences
  (`~/Library/Preferences/dev.korchasa.SmartLinksOpener.plist`).
- Mac App Store (sandboxed) build: inside the app's sandbox container
  (`~/Library/Containers/dev.korchasa.SmartLinksOpener/`).

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

## Links opened in browsers

When you click a link, the app hands the URL to the browser you chose (directly,
or via your saved rule). The app does not log, store, or transmit your browsing
history; it only routes the link to the selected browser at that moment.

## App Store Connect privacy label

This corresponds to **"Data Not Collected"** in App Store Connect.
